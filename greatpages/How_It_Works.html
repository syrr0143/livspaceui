Frequently Asked Questions

1. I’m confused. What’s a project and what’s an order?

Homes designed and executed for homeowners such as yourself is a project. And usually, every home project requires a combination of civil work plus catalogue and custom products. These tasks are specific orders which depend upon the scope of work involved. So, a project has at least one order within it. And, the orders are what you’re ultimately billed for.

2. Tell me about your catalogue products

To make your life easy, we have curated over 2.5 lakh products onto our catalogue. We’ve Livspace branded products as well as some of the best-in-category brands from India and abroad such as Gulmohar Lane, Rug Republic, Hettich and Hafele to name a few. From sofas, beds, chairs, tables, lighting and appliances to kitchens, wardrobes and storage units, you can find anything and everything for your home project here.

3. What services do you offer? Why should I choose them?

Livspace services include civil work, custom furniture, false ceiling, flooring, tiling, cladding and so much more.

We consistently deliver high-quality interiors because we’ve a 100% in-house team, whose work we stringently monitor based on a 146-quality checklist. And that’s why we can even give you a full home warranty!

4. I understand orders, but what is the execution milestone?

At the execution milestone, 40 - 50% of the work in terms of the order value, is completed. Once the order reaches this milestone, the balance payment is due.

6. Do you charge a design fee?

Yes, we do have an 12% design and management fee. This fee covers the services of your designer, your project manager, and much more!

7. Can I get just get a part of my home designed?

Of course! We’d be happy to design just a part of your home for you - say a modular kitchen or living room or your kid’s bedroom. Get in touch with our designers. They can guide you through the process.

8. Is my online payment secure?

Yes! We’ve taken every measure to protect you from digital payment frauds. With every order, you will receive a secure online payment link. Alternatively, you can also pay through cash, cheque or wire transfer.

9. I want to do my interiors. But I can't afford it right away...

Don’t worry. At Livspace, you can design now and pay later. We’ve partnered with leading financial institutions such as ICICI bank, Yes bank and LiquiLoans for easy monthly installments. Contact your designer to know more.

10. I’m afraid I’ll have to cancel my project. Will I get a refund?

We’re sorry to see you go. But you sure will get a full refund if you are cancelling your project within 48 hours of paying the booking fee. More details on our cancellation policy, can be found here.

11. Do you offer any warranty?

Yes, we’re India’s only home interiors platform to give you a full home warranty! This means when you design your interiors with us, you get flat 10-years warranty on our catalogue products, and a 1-year warranty on our services.
