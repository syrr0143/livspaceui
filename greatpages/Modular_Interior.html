FAQs

What is the difference between Livspace Select and Livspace Vesta?

It’s kind of like a personal pizza to a large one. Same quality, different portion sizes. Livspace Select offers you the convenience of setting up different areas of your home quickly. We do this using modular solutions for sections like kitchens, wardrobes and storage customised to meet your requirements. Livspace Vesta on the other hand is ideal for customers looking for a complete makeover, starting from scratch and creating a wholesome design for the entire home.

What can I expect to spend if I were to choose Livspace Select?

Again, like in a pizza - it depends on the toppings. Typically though most Livspace Select projects tend to range from ₹2-10 lakhs.

Is this a safe time to do my home interiors?

We sure understand your concern. Which is why we have implemented a strict no-contact policy. With temperature checks, fumigation, online meetings, and all other necessary safety protocols, we’ve got your safety well covered. So let go of your worries and kick start your dream today.

How is Livspace different from contractors/individual designers/studios?

It’s almost like night and day. On one hand, you have extensive self-research, numerous market trips trying to choose materials, finishes and furniture, and the endless confusion of co-ordinating work on site. On the other hand, you have the bright experience Livspace offers with its cutting-edge technology. We give you everything you’ll ever need — from top designers, expert modular installation partners, reliable warranties and much more.

What are the cities that Livspace currently operates in?

We are operational in over 60+ cities in India, and we’re spreading real fast. Today, you can find us in all Tier 1 cities, most of the Tier 2 cities and we have recently started operating in Tier 3 cities as well.

Can I get just a part of my home designed?

Your dream is our dream. Small or big, we’re equally excited - even if that means just getting a modular kitchen. However, to make it worth the time and effort, we’ve set a minimum budget of ₹2 lakhs. The simplest way to find out if your scope of work fits, is to have a quick conversation with one of our designers.

Can I cancel my booking?

We truly believe you wouldn’t want to. But in the unlikely event that you do, you sure can within 7 days after paying the booking amount, for a full refund. Considering the effort and time your personal design team invests for you, we would be unable to offer a refund after 7 days. We will however be able to cancel or replace specific products in your projects until the end of the design phase.
